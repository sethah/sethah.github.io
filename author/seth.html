Finally understanding kernels and the kernel trick

Date Thu 31 August 2017 By Seth Category posts. Tags ml kernels bases

I've struggled to really understand Support Vector Machines (SVMs) pretty much ever since I was introduced to them. The rudimentary explanation - they are a maximum-margin classifier - makes good enough sense, and for much of data science, that knowledge, coupled with knowing when to apply an SVM to a particular problem, can probably get you by. Still, it doesn't feel great to have so much confusion surrounding something so fundamental to machine learning as SVMs.
